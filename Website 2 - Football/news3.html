Manchester City bite the bullet on Southampton and see a fantastic series end

PUBLISHED: 09:27 GMT, 22 January 2022

Manchester City have lost points in the Premier League for the first time since October 30. The team of manager Pep Guardiola bit itself at Southampton on Saturday: 1-1.

Manchester City seems to be heading for the league title at lightning speed this season. The Citizens have won their last twelve league matches and thus took a significant lead over the competition. However, the visit to Southampton did not go as planned. The home side took the lead after seven minutes via Kyle Walker-Peters, who set up and completed the attack himself.

It was one of The Saints' few feats of arms, but for a long time it seemed enough for a stunt. Raheem Sterling, among others, was too laconic in the completion and failed to equalize Manchester City. In the second half, the leader shifted up a gear, but former Vitesse player Broja hit the post. Aymeric Laporte, on the other hand, had more luck with the head, from a free kick by Kevin De Bruyne.

Guardiola's formation went looking for more and saw De Bruyne hit the post with a beautiful shot. The aluminum then also got in the way of a goal from Gabriel Jesus, which meant City again dropped points. Liverpool can reduce the deficit to nine points on Sunday.
