Tagliafico 'with the plague in the body at Ajax': 'He wants to leave because he does not play'

PUBLISHED: 10:50 GMT, 22 January 2022

Nicol√°s Tagliafico wants to leave Ajax in January, but trainer Erik ten Hag prefers not to lose the Argentinian. The issue is causing division within the club, as Ajax can use the money for the left-back.

Ajax watcher Mike Verweij of De Telegraaf announces that there are different changes within Ajax about the Tagliafico situation. "It is labeled within Ajax as a bad position," Verweij says in De Telegraaf's Kick-off Podcast. "Tagliafico is a detainee within the club. It is often the case that Ajax says: if a player has been loyal in the past, we will cooperate on a transfer. That would already apply to Tagliafico last summer, of course for the right price. "

Now that the interest in the Argentine back is there, the player himself hopes for a transfer. "Different clubs present themselves during the winter break. Barcelona is even mentioned, Napoli, Olympique Marseille have been serious. There is a lot of interest, but Ten Hag rightly says: I will fight to keep him. He only has seven defenders and does not want to lose Tagliafico," said Verweij.

Ten Hag stands directly opposite the leadership of the club. "For the club management, it is probably an option to sell Tagliafico, because of the financial situation. I think it's Russian roulette if you do that, but that will have to be decided by Ajax", Verweij, who notes that Tagliafico's agent has ended the week in Amsterdam. "In the conversations with the club, it became clear once again that Tagliico has the plague in his body at Ajax. He wants to leave because he does not play."
