Adventure golf

ADEVNTURE GOLF (aka: Mini-Golf, Putt Putt, Goofy Golf)

is an offshoot of the sport of golf focusing solely on the putting aspect of its parent game. The aim of the game is to score the lowest number of points. It is played on courses consisting of a series of holes (usually a multiple of 9) similar to its parent, but characterized by their short length (usually within 10 yards from tee to cup).
